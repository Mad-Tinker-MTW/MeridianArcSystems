# Meridian Knowledge Specification v0.6 RC1

**Status:** Accepted Foundation Release  
**Frozen:** 2026-07-24  
**Scope:** 125 Foundation assets

## Candidate decision

MKS v0.6 RC1 passed every acceptance criterion and was accepted as the MKS v0.6
Foundation Release. It freezes the validated Foundation behind ten acceptance
gates and separates release requirements from post-release ambition.

## Included

- 48 specification objects
- 27 glossary terms
- 25 worked applications
- 10 Academy learning paths
- 5 connected platform capabilities
- 3 governed reader releases
- 10 of 10 hardening gates passed
- 8 of 8 validation missions documented
- 94/100 external usability score, one retained friction finding, zero blockers

## Remediation since v0.5

- Clarified what counts as a knowledge object in VAL-001.
- Replaced “Authorize movement” with “Decide the next move.”
- Strengthened contrast on light-background sections.
- Added mobile clearance beneath the fixed header.
- Published external validation as governed release REL-003.

## Known limitations

- Independent human testing is real but still small.
- Human-live and AI source-reviewed evidence remain separately attributed.
- Formal certification and commercial packaging are outside RC1.
- Large-audience rate limiting remains post-RC infrastructure work.

## Acceptance rule

RC1 passed final review and became v0.6 on 2026-07-24. New ideas do not reopen
the released scope; they enter a later version.
